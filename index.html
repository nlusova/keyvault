Questions?

Are these genuine Microsoft licenses? +
Yes. All keys are 100% genuine and come directly from authorized distributors. They activate successfully on Microsoft servers.
How fast will I receive my key? +
Delivery is fully automated. Your license key will arrive in your inbox within 60 seconds of purchase — 24/7, including weekends.
What if my key doesn't work? +
We offer a full replacement guarantee. If your key fails to activate, contact our support and we'll send a replacement immediately.
Can I use the key on multiple devices? +
Each license is for one device. If you need multiple licenses, simply place multiple orders.
How do I activate my Windows? +
Go to Settings → Update & Security → Activation → Change product key. Enter your key and follow the instructions. Internet connection required.
